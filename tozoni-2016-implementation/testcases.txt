agp2009a-orthorand (600 files)
20 vertex-count classes N ∈ {20, 40, 60, 80, 100, 200, 300, …, 2500} (same list as before).
30 random instances per class (second number in the name).
Pattern: random-<N>-<id>.pol → 20 × 30 = 600.


agp2009a-simplerand (600 files)
Same structure as orthorand: 20 sizes × 30 ids.
Pattern: randsimple-<N>-<id>.pol → 600.

agp2009a-rvk (480 files)
16 sizes × 30 ids (sizes stop at 1500, no 1750–2500).
Pattern: randvon-<N>-<id>.pol → 16 × 30 = 480.

gB-simple-simple (300 files)
10 different grid/geometry settings (the …_<min>:<max>v-<h>h part in the name, e.g. 25:100v-10h, …, 250:1000v-100h).
30 numbered instances per setting (_1.pol … _30.pol).
Pattern: gB_simple-simple_<spec>_<k>.pol → 10 × 30 = 300.

gD-ortho-ortho (300 files)
Same idea as gB: 10 geometry specs × 30 instances each.
Pattern: gD_ortho-ortho_<spec>_<k>.pol → 300.
